Who's Summrs?

image via pintrest.com

Summrs is an American rapper from Louisiana.
His real is Deante Adam Johnson and he was born
November 18, 1999. He rose to prominence as a
pioneering figure in the "Pluggnb" subgenre in 2018.
Summrs still continues to drop Pluggnb Music along
with Glo and the Lousiana-YoungBoy style music.
